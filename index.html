Zucorians in the Community

Zucorians have a lot of great qualities in common, but one of the most important qualities we share, is our passion for giving back to our community by supporting not-for-profit organizations such as Junior Achievment South Western Ontario Habitit for Humanity, Diabetes Canada, and London Community Foundation.

As part of our belief that business is not just about numbers, we support nationwide movements that positively impact our industry while keeping people at the heart of our misssion.

The Retail Sales Professional Award program is an annual celebration that recognizes excellence in Canada's Home Furnishings Industry. By celebration outstanding achievment on a peer-recognized basis, the RSP Awards acknowledge the industry sales professionals who go above and beyond to deliver service excellence.
